Noodles

Preparation Time: 20 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Fry vegetables
  3. Add noodles
  4. Mix and serve

Chef Tips

Don't overcook noodles.

Nutrition

Calories: 220 kcal

Serving

Serve hot with sauce.
