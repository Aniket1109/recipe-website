Biryani

Preparation Time: 60 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Prepare chicken gravy
  3. Layer rice and chicken
  4. Cook on low flame

Chef Tips

Use basmati rice for best taste.

Nutrition

Calories: 400 kcal

Serving

Serve with raita.
